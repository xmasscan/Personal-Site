Hello! I am Michael Hanif Khan, an Undergraduate Computer Science major currently enrolled at The University of Illinois at Chicago.

For as long as I can remember, I've always been interested in the how and why of things work. As such, I am interested in a large range of topics outside of Computer Science, such as Philosophy, Psychology, Physics, Electronics, and Engineering, to name a few. Although I am nowhere as knowledgable in these fields as I am in Computer Science, I am always interested in interdisiplinary studies or work which take advantage of math and computer science to answer new questions.

Ultimately what draws me to Computer Science more than any other field is its logical, mathematical roots, its expansive potential, and its propensity for change.
